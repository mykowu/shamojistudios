Counting Numbers with Nom Nom Fish

ebook available on Amazon Kindle

(if you would like to purchase a hard cover book, please sign up and we will let you know when it becomes available)

Nom Nom Fish love playing with numbers and they have a lot of fun doing it. In their first book for kids, parents and anyone else who love to learn, our Nom Nom Fish display creative ways to go from 1 to 10.

Join in and play along as they show off individual traits, create shapes and play games for your whole family.

Nom Nom Fish are happy, funny looking and way too smart for their own good, but they love to teach and help young kids get started on a fantastic future.
